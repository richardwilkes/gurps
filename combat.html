Combat Options

Description
Page
Deceptive Attack
Target suffers a -1 to active defense for every -2 taken to combat skill. May not reduce his combat skill below 10.
B369
Dual-Weapon Attack
Use both hands to make two attacks, both at -4, to replace one normal attack (not an All-Out Attack). Off-hand is at the usual -4 for weapon attacks. Can target separate foes (if adjacent for melee). Foe defends at -1 if he is the target of both attacks.
B417
Rapid Strike
Make two attacks (with a ready hand/weapon), both at -6, to replace one normal attack. Can target separate foes (within a 30° angle for Ranged). Ranged attacks require a weapon with RoF of 2+. Divide the RoF evenly into two separate attacks, and use each to determine the rapid-fire bonus.
